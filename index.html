Introduction

We are an entirely student run volunteer organization. Our program seeks to inspire students to pursue STEM through engaging presentations and hands-on activities and labs. We volunteer at Carolyn Clark Elementary School every other Wednesday.

About Us

IST was founded by three current juniors at EVHS with the aim of inspiring students to expore science, technology, engineering, and math related fields. We have a deep passion for STEM, and wanted to give back to our community through this program.

Our Program

Integrated Science and Technology aims to inspire students to pursue STEM in a unique, hands-on approach. Each session consists of three hands on components: an engaging demonstration to interest students, a presntation about a relevant STEM topic, and a lab experiment to allow studens to apply the knowledge they have learned.

Some of our previous labs include physical and chemical changes with lava lamps, chemical reactions with gel and salt, and biological compounds with a fruit DNA extraction lab.
